1. Information We Collect

Label Wise does not require account creation or login. We do not ask users to submit their name, email address, phone number, or password in order to use the app.

Depending on how the app is used, the app may process:

  • Camera data: used when you choose barcode scanning or label-photo features.
  • Food product data: such as barcode numbers, product names, ingredient lists, nutrition details, allergens, additives, and related label information.
  • User preference data: selections such as religion, ethical preferences, medical or condition-related preferences, and lifestyle goals.
  • Device-generated installation identifier: a random installation ID created on your device to support app and backend functions.
  • Approximate network-based location context: the app may use an IP-based lookup to infer approximate country context. The app does not request GPS or precise location permission.
  • Locally stored usage data: including preferences and scan history saved on your device.

2. How We Use Information

We use processed information to:

  • scan and analyze food labels and packaged products;
  • generate AI-assisted food assessment results and recommendations;
  • save preferences and scan history on your device;
  • retrieve product details from public food databases;
  • support backend operations tied to your installation of the app;
  • improve app quality, model behavior, and analysis workflows.

3. When Information Is Shared With Services

Label Wise communicates with third-party and backend services in order to function. Based on the current app behavior, this can include:

  • OpenAI: prompts and, when applicable, product label image data may be sent to generate AI-based analysis and recommendations.
  • Open Food Facts: barcode numbers may be sent to retrieve public product information.
  • IP geolocation provider: a request may be made to infer an approximate country from your network connection.
  • Label Wise backend: the app may send installation ID, app and platform information, product analysis inputs, selected preferences, and generated result payloads to support operations and improvement.

These services may process data under their own policies, terms, and retention practices.

4. Data Storage

Some data is stored locally on your device, including preferences, scan history, installation ID, and related app state. Data sent to remote services may also be stored, logged, or processed by those services according to their own operational needs and policies.

5. Data Retention

On-device data is generally retained until you clear app data, remove saved history inside the app where available, or uninstall the app. Remote-service data may be retained as needed for app functionality, security, debugging, product improvement, or legal compliance.

6. Your Choices

  • You may stop using camera-based features at any time.
  • You may remove locally saved history using in-app controls where available.
  • You may uninstall the app to remove locally stored app data from your device.

7. Children

Label Wise is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly design or market the app for children under 13.

8. Medical Disclaimer

Label Wise provides informational and AI-assisted food label assessment only. It is not a medical device, not a diagnostic service, and not a substitute for professional medical, nutritional, allergy-related, or healthcare advice.

9. Security

We use reasonable efforts to protect data processed by the app. However, no method of storage, device protection, or internet transmission is completely secure.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If material changes are made, the effective date on this page will be updated.
